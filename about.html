Our History & Mission

Founded in 1995, Excel Heights High School has a rich tradition of academic excellence. We started with a single building and a vision to provide holistic education.

Our Mission: To cultivate a community of lifelong learners who are compassionate, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens.

Our Vision: To be the leading institution in secondary education, recognized for innovation and student success.
